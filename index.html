Gameplay Guide

Master the art of screw puzzles with our step-by-step guide

Gameplay Steps

1

Observe the Structure

Start by examining the 3D object and identifying the different colored screws and their positions. Take your time to understand the puzzle layout.

Multiple colors
2

Strategic Removal

Carefully remove screws, one at a time, ensuring you don't create blockages or make moves that hinder future progress. Think before you unscrew!

Click to remove screws
3

Matching Colors

Move the removed screws into the designated trays, making sure each tray only contains screws of the same color. Organization is key!

Red tray
4

Unlock Levels

As you successfully sort the screws and complete levels, you'll unlock new, more challenging 3D structures and puzzles.

Unlock new challenges
5

Utilize Tools

The game may offer tools like extra trays or a holding pen for screws, which can be obtained through in-game currency or by watching ads.

Use tools strategically

Tips for Success

Plan Your Moves

Before removing a screw, consider the potential consequences for other screws and the overall structure. Think 2-3 moves ahead.

Mind the Order

The order in which you remove screws is crucial for avoiding blockages. Sometimes removing an easily accessible screw first can create problems later.

Use Tools Wisely

If available, use extra trays or the holding pen to manage screws effectively. Save your tools for when you really need them.

Master Observation

Pay close attention to the colors and positions of screws to develop effective sorting strategies. Zoom in and rotate the structure for better views.

Enjoy the Challenge

Screwdom 3D is designed to be relaxing yet engaging. Take your time and enjoy the process of solving each level.
