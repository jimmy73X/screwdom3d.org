Frequently Asked Questions

Is Screwdom 3D completely free to play?

Yes, Screwdom 3D is completely free to play in your browser with no downloads required.

Do I need to create an account to play?

No, you can play without an account. However, creating an account allows you to save your progress.

How often are new levels added?

We add new levels regularly, typically every 2-3 months, to keep the game fresh and challenging.

Can I play on mobile devices?

Yes, Screwdom 3D is optimized for both desktop and mobile browsers.
